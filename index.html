Spring User Group

Le Spring User Group Paris rassemble et met en relation les personnes intéressées par les technologies Spring. Venez y partager votre enthousiasme, vos connaissances, vos questions autour de Spring et participer aux conférences organisées.

Spring User Group Paris allows you to stay in touch with local and global other Spring-ecosystem actors. Join us to share your enthusiasm, your knowledge and any Spring-related question you might have! And of course, you are more than welcome to submit a talk.
